There is no catch. We don't charge you a £60/month retainer just to keep your site online. You pay £197 once for the build. *You only pay a standard registrar fee (about £9/year) directly to your provider for your domain name (e.g. yourname.co.uk), which you own entirely.

Do I show up on Google?

Yes. We build your site with local SEO in mind, and more importantly, we set up your Google Business Profile. This is what puts you on the local map pack when people search for your trade in your local area.

What if I need updates later?

If you need minor text updates or new photos added down the line, just WhatsApp us. We charge a simple, small one-off fee for the specific updates you need, rather than trapping you in a monthly maintenance contract you rarely use.
